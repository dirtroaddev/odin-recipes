Pizza Recipe

A Pizza Pie

This is a recipe that shows you how to make a wonderfuly delightful pepperoni pizza!!

It includes a rich, robust sauce and a golden, flaky crust

Ingredients:

  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Sauce
  • Pizza Dough

Steps:

  1. Lay out the dough
  2. Use a spoon to spread out the sauce on the dough make it even
  3. Place the shredded mozzerella cheese evenly over the entire inside pizza, not including the crust
  4. Place the pepperoni on top of the cheese
  5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit
  6. Place piza in oven and cook for 22 minutes
